# Buzzword Olympics Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24 July 2026
rivacy Policy

Effective Date: 24 July 2026

Buzzword Olympics is developed and published by Big Diz Apps ("we", "our", or "us").

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information our app collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect:

Information You Provide
Display name or nickname used within the app
Custom game names
Custom buzzword packs created by you
Feedback or support emails you send to us
Automatically Collected Information

To provide and improve the app, we may collect:

Anonymous device identifiers
App version
Device type
Operating system version
Crash reports
Performance diagnostics
Basic gameplay statistics
Anonymous usage analytics

This information does not identify you personally.

Multiplayer Games

Buzzword Olympics allows players to participate in multiplayer games.

To support multiplayer gameplay we temporarily store information including:

Player display names
Join codes
Game progress
Winners
Leaderboard results

Completed games are automatically removed after a limited period unless required for long-term statistics or achievements.

Firebase

Buzzword Olympics uses Google Firebase to provide services including:

Anonymous Authentication
Cloud Firestore
Multiplayer game synchronisation
Player profiles
Leaderboards
Cloud data storage

Firebase processes data according to Google's Privacy Policy.

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Advertising

The free version of Buzzword Olympics displays advertisements using Google AdMob.

AdMob may collect information such as:

Device identifiers
Advertising identifiers (where permitted)
Approximate location derived from IP address
Diagnostic information
Advertising performance data

Where required by law, users will be asked for consent before personalised advertising is shown.

Users who purchase Premium Pass or Presenter Pass will not be shown advertisements.

In-App Purchases

Buzzword Olympics offers optional in-app purchases processed securely through Apple's App Store.

We do not receive or store your payment information.

Apple's Privacy Policy:

https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Analytics

We may use anonymous analytics to understand:

Which features are most popular
App stability
Crash information
General gameplay trends

This information helps improve future updates.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information using industry-standard security measures.

However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

Buzzword Olympics is suitable for schools and families.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will remove it where appropriate.

Your Choices

You may:

Stop using the app at any time
Delete the app
Contact us to request deletion of stored profile information where applicable
Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be published at this page with an updated effective date.
